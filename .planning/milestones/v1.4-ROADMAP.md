## Milestone Summary

**Key Decisions:**
- Primary blue #1152d4 replacing gold #d4a017 as wireframe accent color
- color-mix(in srgb) for semi-transparent fills (Tailwind opacity modifiers don't work with CSS var hex)
- Dark sidebar in both light and dark themes (--wf-sidebar-bg: #0f172a always)
- CompositionBar uses pure HTML/CSS flex (no Recharts dependency)

**Requirements:** TOK-01 through TOK-07, SIDE-01 through SIDE-05, HEAD-01 through HEAD-04, CARD-01 through CARD-05, TBL-01 through TBL-05, FILT-01 through FILT-05, CHRT-01 through CHRT-05, EDIT-01, GAL-01, GAL-02 — all shipped.
