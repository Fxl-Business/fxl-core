## Decisions Made
- **Primary color:** Cool dark gray-blue (HSL 220 16% 22%) instead of fxl-navy. More sophisticated, works in both modes.
- **Gold accent:** HSL 43 96% 56% for light mode (warm, premium), HSL 43 85% 50% for dark mode (slightly desaturated for comfort).
- **Sidebar active state:** Chose `bg-primary/10 text-primary` pattern over the filled `bg-fxl-navy text-white` approach. Subtle, production-grade, works in both light and dark modes without requiring separate dark styling.
- **Logo badge:** Uses `bg-primary` semantic token rather than any hardcoded brand color, ensuring it adapts to both themes.
- **Layout background:** Clean `bg-background` replacing the hardcoded light-only gradient. Simple and correct for both modes.
